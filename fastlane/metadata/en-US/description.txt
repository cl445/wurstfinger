Wurstfinger - The Keyboard for Fat Fingers

Tired of typos? Wurstfinger is an innovative iOS keyboard designed specifically for one-handed use. Inspired by the proven MessagEase layout, it offers large keys and intuitive gestures for fast, accurate typing.

FEATURES:

• Large Keys - Fewer mistakes, more comfort
• Swipe Gestures - Type letters by swiping in 8 directions
• 14 Languages - English, German, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Polish, Czech, and Vietnamese
• Compose Engine - Create accents and special characters through combinations (e.g. ' + a = á)
• Circular Gestures - Uppercase letters through circular motions
• Cursor Control - Move the cursor by dragging on the space bar
• Haptic Feedback - Adjustable vibration intensity
• Customizable - Scale the keyboard to your preferences

PERFECT FOR:

• One-handed use on the go
• Users with larger fingers
• Anyone looking for an ergonomic alternative to the standard keyboard

Wurstfinger requires a short learning curve, but once you've mastered the gestures, you'll type faster and more accurately than ever before.
