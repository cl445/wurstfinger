To test the Wurstfinger keyboard:

1. Install the app and open it
2. Follow the in-app setup guide, or manually:
   - Open Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards
   - Tap "Add New Keyboard..."
   - Select "Wurstfinger"
3. Optionally enable "Allow Full Access" for haptic feedback:
   - Tap "Wurstfinger" in the keyboard list
   - Toggle "Allow Full Access"
   - Note: Full Access is only used for haptic feedback, no data is collected
4. Open any app with text input (e.g., Notes, Messages)
5. Tap the globe icon (üåê) on the keyboard to switch to Wurstfinger
6. Type by tapping the center of keys, or swipe in 8 directions for additional characters

Gesture tips:
- Swipe from center outward for secondary characters
- Draw a circle on a letter key for uppercase
- Drag on the space bar to move the cursor
- Drag left on the delete key for continuous deletion
